ABOUT US

Bookshop was established in 1975. A humble start from a small bookstall to medium size book shop – we always strive to drop down the illiteracy level in Pakistan. We proudly deliver books online all across Karachi. We are on the way to provide books all across Pakistan at an economical price. So, no one remains deprived of education. We sell Urdu Bazar online books in Karachi. We started our journey from the Urdu Bazar Karachi; the Hub of booksellers in the metropolitan city. We are selling old as well as new books at a cost-effective price. At our bookstall, you can buy a wide array of second hand at a 50% price. From O Level to A level or other Cambridge we sell all kinds of books.

AND MISSION

Bookshop is not just a place to buy books, we also provide mentorship to parents for their child’s education. Because some of the parents become anxious about their child's education. Undoubtedly, the school has a great impact on a child. We help the parent in choosing the best school. Our aim or mission is not bounded around buying, and selling the book. We want to eliminate illiteracy from Pakistan. We show people a path to find a better school for their child.Bookshop is not just a medium-size book shop in Urdu Bazar Karachi, we work as a mentor for those parents who desire to give their children the best education. We deal in second-hand books to eliminate illiteracy from Pakistan.
